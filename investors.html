Roadmap For Investors

For investors.

Most of the useful AI being built in the next few years won't live inside a megamodel. It'll live next to private data. We're building the platform for it.

What we're building

A Resident is a small AI trained on a single dataset for a single job. You own it. You can version it, test it, and ship it where your data already lives — a laptop, a private cloud, an air-gapped lab.

Not a prompt. Not a wrapper. A computer.

Why now

Two things shifted in the last eighteen months. Training a small, specialist model has gotten roughly an order of magnitude cheaper. And the institutions sitting on the most valuable data — hospitals, law firms, regulated labs, defence — increasingly can't send it to a third-party API, even if they want to.

So the interesting work is moving to the edge: closer to the data, away from the giant general-purpose models. We think this is a category. We want to build it.

Where we are

We're at concept stage. The core architecture is in place. The first Residents are running in private.
